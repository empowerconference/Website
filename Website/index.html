1st EMPOWER Assistive Technology Conference

EMPOWER 2018 is being organized to bring together all stakeholders in the assistive technology sector. This includes researchers, user organizations, industries as well as policy makers engaged in different ways with the issues involved in creation, production and dissemination of assistive technology solutions. This three day event should see active participation of all stakeholders not only to discuss current solutions but also to seed ideas for research groups to work on. The event would consist of tutorials, key note talks, invited papers, submitted and reviewed papers, posters, user forums, panel discussion as well as an exhibition.

Venue

IIT Techno Park
Rajiv Gandhi Educational City
SH 11, Khewra, Haryana 131021
